Gorka Fraga González

I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Developmental Psychology of the University of Amsterdam. My main research interest is language development and the underlying neurophysiological processes. I am interested in combining behavioral and neuroimaging studies to better understand how language learning works, and how to improve current educational/remedial programs.
